Terminal Maps

The beautiful SRQ terminal building is a combination of efficiency, comfort and aesthetics, with a distinct local flavor.

For your convenience, the terminal features very short walking distances, with a simple progression from the parking lot, to check-in counters, to security screening and onto the concourse. For arriving passengers, the distances are equally as short from the gates. Walking to the baggage claim area from an arrival gate takes only a few minutes; once there you will find car rental counters. The parking lot and ground transportation are easily accessible adjacent to the baggage claim area.

Getting to SRQ

Address: 6000 Airport Circle Sarasota, FL 34241

Sarasota Bradenton International Airport is located at 6000 Airport Circle, Sarasota, Florida 34243 Phone: 941-359-2770 ext. 4300.


Located on the Southwest coast of Florida, SRQ is centered between Tampa Bay and Fort Myers. To reach the airport from I-75 take Exit 213, University Parkway westbound. The airport is 6.5 miles west of the interstate on the north side of University Parkway.


From Sarasota, proceed North on U.S. 41/Tamiami Trail to University Parkway, turn right, then immediately merge to the left. Turn left at the first traffic signal at the airport entrance.


From Bradenton, proceed South on U.S. 41/Tamiami Trail to University Parkway, turn left, then immediately merge to the left. Turn left at the first traffic signal at the airport entrance.


The Florida Department of Transportation’s (FDOT) Florida 511 Traveler Information System provides real-time traffic and travel information to help people arrive safely and on time to their destination: Know before you go and visit FL511.com



SRQ Parking

Short-term parking is available directly across the terminal building. There is a free parking shuttle operating between the Long-term lot, and all overflow parking lots once Long-term is full. While we do not run out of spaces, parking lots do fill up and overflow parking opens when needed with shuttle service to the terminal. Please plan your trip to SRQ accordingly. The shuttle service to the front of the terminal is available from two hours prior to the first morning departure until after the last scheduled arriving flight, even if delayed. American Express, VISA, MasterCard, and Discover credit cards are accepted.

SRQ offers on-site car rentals with counters conveniently located in the baggage claim area. Rental cars can be picked up and returned at the covered rental car lot within walking distance of the terminal building. Listed below are the companies and their contact information.

Over a dozen rental companies are available including: Alamo, Avis, Budget, Enterprise, and Hertz

Public Transit

Public transit services are available in the Ground Transportation area located at the west end of the baggage claim area. Buses from both Manatee and Sarasota counties make regular stops to downtown Sarasota and downtown Bradenton . You may contact the transit companies directly for schedule and fare information..

View bus schedules below:

  • Sarasota County Area Transit (SCAT) or call 941-861-5000
  • Manatee County Area Transit (MCAT) or call 941-749-7116

Ride App Pickup

 

Guests departing the Airport using Uber or Lyft can be picked up by the service provider at Red Baggage Claim, the Blue Express Curbside, or the Remote Curbisde at the SkyCenter One office building. A service fee of $5.00 will be added to the cost of the fare. Guests may be dropped off at any public access location including Ticketing, Baggage Claim or the Remote Curbside at the SkyCenter One office building.

Luxury Sedan and SUV Service

On-Demand Eco-friendly Sedans, Luxury Sedans, and SUVs are available just outside the baggage claim area on the west end of the terminal. SRQ’s select on-demand transportation is provided by ALD Limo and is available for all arriving flights. A uniformed starter will connect you with a luxury vehicle of your choice, provide you with a fixed rate, based on your destination, load your luggage, and get you on your way. Download the ALD Limo free mobile app to find guaranteed rates. You may contact ALD Limo Premium Limousine Service at 855-799-LIMO, or via ALD Limo.


COVID-19 Update: ALD Limo has installed a new layer of protection inside every vehicle – sneeze guards. The protective screens will stand between a passenger and a driver. Vehicles are sanitized after each customer and face masks are required for drivers.
